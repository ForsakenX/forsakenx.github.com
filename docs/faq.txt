- ProjectX is an updated (bug fixes, features, system port) version of Acclaim Forsaken.

- It does not require the old cd and is consists of a single download.

- There is no installation, just unzip and run the game.

- To host/join multiplayer games you must open your UDP port 2300 on your firewall/router. 

- To download the game and for more complete information check out the site (http://fly.thruhere.net). 

- People are usually around from 10am to 10pm EST (-5) to help you further. 

- If no one is around to help, you can email fskn.methods@gmail.com
