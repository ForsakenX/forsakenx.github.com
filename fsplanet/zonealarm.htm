Configuring ZoneAlarm to run with Forsaken:
 

Step 1: Select "Program Control" from the left menu within the ZoneAlarm Control Center.

Step 2: Select the "Programs" tab.

Step 3: Click the "Add" button.

Step 4: Navigate to ...\WINDOWS\system32

Step 5: Select dplaysvr.exe

Step 6: Locate Microsoft DirectPlay Helper from the list of Programs.

Step 7: Left-click on the ? for Trust Level and choose "Super" and then click "yes".

Step 8: Left-click on the remaining ?s for Access and Server and select "Allow" for each.
 

Step 9: Click the "Add" button.

Step 10: Navigate to C:\Program Files\Acclaim Entertainment\Forsaken

Step 11: Select ForsakenHW.exe

Step 12: Repeat steps 6, 7 and 8 for ForsakenHW.exe
