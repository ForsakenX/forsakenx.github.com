Forsaken

Forsaken was developed by Probe Entertainment a division of Acclaim Entertainment in 1995.

Genre

It is generally described as a 6DoF (Degrees of Freedom) first person shooter.

Meaning players can move in any direction and rotate along any axis, without being affected by gravity.

Only a few games of this type have ever been created, namely Forsaken and the Descent series.

Game Play

Forsaken's game play generally aims to be fast, fun, and arcade like.

There is the classic single player mode and online multiplayer gaming.

The mixture of weapons and freedom of motion allows for unique styles and competitive gameplay.

Multiplayer supports free for all and various team / flag modes.

More information on game play can be found on the Wiki and FsPlanet.

Development

It is now actively maintained by the player community under the original code name ProjectX.

The code base is a fork of the final patch release with various bug fixes and new features.

It has been fully ported to run on cross platform technologies and currently officially supports Linux, MacOSX and Windows.

Community

Loosely made of Foraken and Descent players working to build and support the development of 6DoF games.

We also work on prototypes and tooling for both games and new engines we are building.
